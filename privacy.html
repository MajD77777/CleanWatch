Privacy Policy

CleanWatch is a public informational website designed to help users identify sensitive scenes in movies and series before watching.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

CleanWatch does not require user accounts and does not collect personal identification data.

Certain data (such as titles added through the Admin panel) may be stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Cookies

CleanWatch does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Analytics

CleanWatch currently does not use third-party analytics tools.

Third-Party Services

CleanWatch is not affiliated with any streaming service and does not host or stream video content.

Data Security

Since most data is stored locally in your browser, clearing your browser storage may remove saved content.
